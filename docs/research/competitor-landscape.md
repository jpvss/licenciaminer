# Competitive Landscape — LicenciaMiner

Compiled: 2026-03-21

## Key Finding: No direct competitor exists

No product combines cross-agency data (IBAMA + ANM + SEMAD), PDF text extraction from Pareceres Técnicos, and approval rate prediction. The niche is open.

## Direct Competitors

| Competitor | What They Do | Threat | Relationship |
|-----------|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **MapBiomas Monitor Mineração** | 257K ANM processes + satellite land cover. Identifies irregularities (mining in restricted areas). Free, NGO-funded. | 6/10 | Complementary — they do title irregularities, we do licensing outcomes |
| **Jazida.com** | SaaS for mining rights workflow management. Environmental module for tracking YOUR licenses. | 4/10 | Potential partner, not competitor |
| **Ambisis** | Environmental management SaaS (39K+ projects). License tracking, waste management, ESG. | 4/10 | Operational compliance, no analytics |
| **Licentia Digital** | Basic license status dashboards | 2/10 | Not a threat |

## Government AI Initiatives (Watch Closely)

| Initiative | Details | Threat |

| **IBAMA + ABDI "Destrava Brasil"** | R$ 10.28M AI investment. Analyzed 40K+ processes. Internal tool for analysts. | 5/10 |

Both validate market need. Both are internal (for government analysts), not external (for applicants).
